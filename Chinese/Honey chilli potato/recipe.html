HONEY CHILLI POTATO

Description

Honey Chilli Potatoes is a supremely popular Chinese snack that has a huge fan following, particularly in India. It is crunchy, flavorful and awfully delicious. Made with deep fried potatoes coated with chilli sauce, honey and a mixture of garlic, vinegar, salt, pepper and chilli flakes, this Chinese recipe can be made quicky and is ideal for parties and get-togethers.

Recipe

Ingredients


3 cups potato fingers
1 tsp garlic paste
2 tsp chilli powder
2 tsp red chilli sauce
5 tbsp cornflour
salt to taste
oil for deep-frying
1 tbsp oil
1 tbsp finely chopped garlic
1 tbsp dry red chilli flakes
2 tsp vinegar
2 tbsp honey
1/2 tsp sugar

For The Garnish
1 tbsp finely chopped coriander
2 tbsp finely chopped spring onion greens

Directions


1.Combine the potatoes, garlic paste, chili powder,1 tsp red chili sauce, corn flour and salt in a deep bowl with mix gently.

2.Heat the oil in a deep non-stick pan, and deep-fry a few potato fingers at time till they turn crisp and golden brown in color from all the sides. Drain on an absorbent paper. Keep aside.

3.Heat oil in a broad non-stick pan, add the garlic and saut√© on a medium flame for a few seconds.

4.Add the chili flakes, the remaining 1 tsp of red chili sauce and vinegar, mix well and cook on a medium flame for a few seconds.

5.Add the honey, sugar and little salt, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 1 minute.

6.Combine the deep-fried potato fingers and the prepared sauce in a deep bowl and toss well.

7.Serve immediately garnished with coriander and spring onion greens.
