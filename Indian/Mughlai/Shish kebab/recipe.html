SHISH KEBAB

Description

Shish kebab (Turkish: şiş kebap; Persian/Mazandarani: شیش کباب, shish kebab) or Seekh kebab (Urdu: سیخ کباب‎) is a popular meal of skewered and grilled cubes of meat. The word kebab denotes a wide variety of different grilled meat dishes. Shish kebab is popular in the whole of Asia.[citation needed] It is similar to a dish called shashlik, which is found in the Caucasus region. It is generally made of lamb (kuzu şiş) but there are also versions with beef or veal (dana şiş), swordfish (kılıç şiş) and chicken meat (tavuk şiş or şiş tavuk). In Turkey, shish kebab and the vegetables served with it are grilled separately, normally not on the same skewer.

Recipe

Ingredients



150 gm mutton minced(keema)
100 gm chicken minced
2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp onion paste
1 tsp red chilli powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1 tsp cumin powder
3/4 tsp powdered pepper
1 tsp dried mango powder
1/4 tsp saunth (Dry ginger powder)
2 tsp oil
1 1/2 Tbsp cashew paste
1 1/2 Tbsp cream
2 Tbsp besan
1 egg yolk
Salt, to taste
Coriander leaves, to garnish

Directions


1.In a bowl mix together the mutton mince and the chicken mince.
2.Mix them with your hands.To that add ginger-garlic paste, onion paste, red chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, powdered pepper, dried mango powder, saunth, oil, cashew paste, cream. Mix nicely.Add besan (Chickpea flour) and egg yolk to bind.
3.Mix well.Add salt to taste. Mix. Cover and keep in the fridge for 1 hour.
4.Skewer the kebabs onto oiled skewers and grill or roast till the outside is nicely browned. Baste frequently with oil while grilling.
5.Once the kebabs are cooked, remove to a serving platter and garnish with onion rings, fresh coriander leaves and lemon wedges.
