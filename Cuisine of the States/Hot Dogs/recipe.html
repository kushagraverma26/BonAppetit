HOT DOGS

Description

A hot dog (also spelled hotdog), also known as a frankfurter (sometimes shortened to frank) or wiener, is a cooked sausage, traditionally grilled or steamed and served in a partially sliced bun.

Recipe

Ingredients


1 tomato, chopped
1/4 cup chopped onions
1 jalapeño pepper, chopped
10 slices Bacon
1 pkg. (16 oz.) Wieners
10 hot dog buns
1/4 cup Mayonnaise
1/2 cup Tomato Ketchup
1/4 cup Yellow Mustard

Directions


1.Heat grill to medium heat.

2.Combine tomatoes, onions and peppers.

3.Wrap 1 bacon slice tightly around each wiener, tucking in ends of bacon slices to secure. Grill 8 to 10 min. or until bacon is done and wieners are heated through, turning occasionally. Drain on paper towels. Meanwhile, brush mayo evenly onto both sides of buns, then grill 1 to 2 min. on each side or until lightly toasted on both sides.

4.Fill buns with wieners; top with tomato mixture, ketchup and mustard.
